Ubicación Privilegiada

Sierra de Carapé sits at the crossroads of Uruguay's most sought-after destinations. A short drive to Punta del Este's beaches and nightlife, or to Montevideo's historic charm—yet here, you're surrounded by rolling hills, native wildlife, and the authentic gaucho spirit.

From sunrise to starlight, every moment at Cabaña Carapé is an invitation to discover. Our curated experiences blend adventure with indulgence.

Walk among our Angus herd. Learn about Uruguay's legendary beef production and the gaucho way of life.

Nuestra Filosofía

Gestión del Campo

The Voisin Rational Grazing system is a regenerative approach where cattle rotate through small paddocks in short bursts, allowing pastures to rest and recover. The whole farm is connected via corridors, so the herd moves naturally between areas while we use natural, non-invasive seeding to continuously upgrade our pastures—working with the land, not against it.

We adopted this system during the pandemic, and it has transformed how we work. It allows us to feed more cattle in less space, while dramatically improving biodiversity: the soil receives rich compost from the cattle's manure and waste, building fertility and life back into the land. This is a system that is changing the world—one pasture at a time.

Quiénes Somos

Cabaña Carapé

We fell in love with the Sierras de Carapé—its raw beauty, its silence, its spirit. Cabaña Carapé was born from a desire to share this undiscovered corner of Uruguay with those who seek something different: not just a place to stay, but an experience that blends the glamour of the coast with the authentic adventure of the interior.

Our team—the people who work the land, care for the animals, and welcome our guests—are the heart of this place. They bring generations of knowledge, warmth, and the true meaning of Uruguayan hospitality.
